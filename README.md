# TRH Medical Organisation

## Overview
TRH Medical Organisation is the **Health and Wellness Unit of The Reinvention House (TRH) Ministries Global**. The team provides **on-site medical support, emergency response, wellness education, and community health outreach** during church services, programs, and outreach events.

The platform supports the medical team with tools for **incident reporting, volunteer coordination, health awareness, and emergency communication**, ensuring a safe and supportive environment for all TRH members, workforce personnel, and guests.

---

## Mission
To ensure **timely medical response, preventive health awareness, and holistic wellbeing** for everyone participating in TRH services, events, and community initiatives.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Provide **first aid and emergency medical support** during church services and events.
- Promote **preventive healthcare and wellness awareness** among members and workforce teams.
- Coordinate with **local hospitals and emergency services** during critical situations.
- Conduct **community health outreaches** as part of TRH’s Love-in-Action initiatives.
- Maintain **safe, hygienic, and health-conscious environments** for worship activities.

---

## Core Features
- **Medical Incident Reporting System**  
  Securely log and manage medical incidents during services and events.

- **Volunteer Medical Team Management**  
  Register and coordinate doctors, nurses, and first responders within the church.

- **Workforce Health Check Scheduling**  
  Enable routine health checks such as blood pressure and glucose screening.

- **Emergency Support Access**  
  Quick access to the medical team during and outside church services through integrated communication channels.

- **Wellness Education Hub**  
  Share health tips, wellness campaigns, and preventive care information.

- **Community Health Outreach Tracking**  
  Document and manage outreach programs including screenings and health education.

---

## Service Coverage
The medical team provides on-site support during:

- Sunday Services  
- Wednesday Midweek Services  
- Conferences and Special Programs  
- Concerts and Worship Events  
- Community Outreach Activities

Outside these times, members can reach the medical team through **dedicated emergency communication channels**.

---

## Technology Stack
The platform is built using modern web technologies:

- **Backend:** Node.js  
- **Server Framework:** Express  
- **Runtime:** TypeScript  
- **Database:** Supabase  
- **Environment Management:** dotenv  

---


## Run Locally

**Prerequisites:**  Node.js


1. Install dependencies:
   `npm install`
2. Set the `GEMINI_API_KEY` in [.env.local](.env.local) to your Gemini API key
3. Run the app:
   `npm run dev`
